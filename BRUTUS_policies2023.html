Entry Discounts

UMRoC members receive a 10% discount on all entry types. AURA members receive a 10% discount on standard entries (discounts are cumulative).

Waitlist

BRuTUS & The Knife - For those that miss out on an entry, a waitlist will be established. The price of your entry will be determined by the date you were added to the waitlist.

Withdrawals

If you cannot participate in the event for any reason, entry fee refunds are available in accordance with the below-stated withdrawal dates only. After the stipulated dates, entry fees are non-refundable for withdrawals initiated by the athlete. Requests for refunds must be emailed to the organiser via info@ultramediocre.run

Withdrawal Refunds

Where eligible, please allow up to 30 business days for your refund to be processed. The refund will be issued to the card originally used to pay for the registration. If this card has since been cancelled or has expired it is the athlete’s responsibility to notify us when requesting a refund.

Prior to 11:59pm, 1 September 2023, 75% of your entry fee less processing fee is refundable

From 00:00am, 2 September to 11:59pm, 15 September 2023, 50% of your entry fee less processing fee is refundable

From 00:00am, 16 September to 11:59pm, 29 September 2023 25% of your entry fee less processing fee is refundable

After 11:59pm, 29 September 2023 — No refunds.

Transfer of entry to different race

Transfers between BRuTUS and The Knife races can be requested up until such time as that events are sold out, subject to the following terms:

There will be no processing charge for transfers requested prior to close of early entries (11:59pm 1 September 2023).

Where you request to transfer to the longer distance (The Knife to BRuTUS), you are required to pay the difference in entry fees between the two distances (as per the applicable entry fees on your original day of entry).

Where you request to transfer to the shorter distance (BRuTUS to the The Knife) the difference in entry fees will not be refunded.

To request a transfer from one event to another, you must email us at info@ultramediocre.run on or before the close of entries (11:59pm Tuesday 3 October 2023).

Transfer of entry to another runner

Entry transfer to another runner will be available until the close of entries.

To request a transfer from one person to another, you must email us at info@ultramediocre.run on or before the close of entries (11:59pm Saturday 30 September 2023).

Any request to transfer entries must include the name and contact details of the person taking your entry.

Upon receipt of your transfer request and a $30 processing charge for transfer (payable by either party), you will be withdrawn from the event and the registration will be issued to the person taking your entry.

Any payment for the entry is to be made between the parties involved in the transfer.

COVID-19 Policy

If the event is cancelled due to COVID-19 or some other reason beyond the control of the organisers, entrants will be offered the opportunity to participate in a rescheduled event or transfer entries to the following year.

If the entrant does not wish to participate in a rescheduled event or transfer entries to the following year, we offer a refund in line with the refund policy (if cancelled before 15 September).
